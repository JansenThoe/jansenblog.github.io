Boyolali's Decreasing Rice Fields

Jansen Thoe
18/02/2024

        Recently, the Solo-Jogja toll road in Indonesia, Jawa Tengah, was built and is now operational. The toll road passes through some parts of Boyolali, a nearby city of Solo. Boyolali has lots of rice fields, and building toll roads would require stacking different types and sizes of rocks on the fields. According to detik.com on 28 November of 2022, a total of 95 hectares of rice fields were affected by the toll road. Since many people in Boyolali are farmers, many peopleâ€™s income will decrease.
